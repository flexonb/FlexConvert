Troubleshooting

  • Large files fail: Your browser may run out of memory. Try smaller batches.
  • PDF rendering issues: The file might be password-protected or corrupted.
  • Audio/Video conversion slow: WebAssembly codecs can be CPU intensive. Keep the tab focused.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • ⌘K / Ctrl K: Open Command Palette
  • Drag & drop or paste files into the dropzone
